about.

why trap. ??

Have you ever heard of Stockholm syndrome? Most people have. Well, there's a similar idea that if you're trapped - It can, ironically, be liberating. Maybe you're living at home with your parents. You might feel trapped, but you're not buying groceries or paying for bills.

This reminds us of good hospitality.

The idea that you can walk into a bar like trap. - give up a little freedom to be guided on an experience - and feel the feeling of being free. Free of the hustle, bustle. Free from the multitude of choice of what to do next. Free of the responsibilities of everything on the otherside of that door you just walked in from. At trap. let us look after you.
