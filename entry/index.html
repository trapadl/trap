menu.

Our menu is constantly changing, but we're in constant supply of 5 house cocktails, three beers and two wines.

MENU 01 - TOY SOLDIERS

long before we opened our doors for the first time as trap. cocktail bar, the space we occupy was claimed as a gallery by one of adelaide’s first successful art groups specialising in graffiti and street art known as the toy soldiers. disbanding in the mid-2010's, their commission work can still be found at miscellaneous places around adelaide. for our first official menu, we inspire five characters commonly found in their work and bring them back to life in the space that was once their home.

about.

why trap. ??

There's an idea out there that being in a trap is always a bad thing. We're here to show you that it's not the case. Some people who live at home still think they're trapped - but quite often they're not paying bills, they're not cooking or barely even cleaning - ironically, sounds like freedom to us.

This reminds us of good hospitality.

The idea that you can walk into a bar like trap. - give up a little freedom to be guided on an experience - and feel the feeling of being free. Free from the grind. Free from the multitude of choice of what to do next. Free of the responsibilities of everything on the otherside of that door you just walked in from. At trap. let us look after you.
